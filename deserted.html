Deserted

Description

Deserted was developed initially in 48 hours for Global Game Jam 2015 with myself and a team of 4 others. More information on the Global Game Jam and the team can be found here. The updated version includes minor bug fixes, additional content and many graphical improvements.

A group of executives is plunged into the unknown, when they crash land on a desert island. Can they work together to escape? Or will they turn on each other to evade the dangers lurking in wait? Use the mouse to choose an executive, and guide them around the island with your mouse. Interact with the island, collect resources, and craft them to create a means to escape - or just survive as long as you can.

Controls

  • Use the mouse (left clicking does mostly everything) to choose an executive and perform actions.

  • The inventory at the bottom of the screen shows you the items available to every executive on the island.

  • The action bar at the bottom of the screen shows all currently available actions.

Help/Tips

  • Find some food and water! You're executives are too used to luxury food...

  • Beware of spiders and caves, you might get hurt!

  • The tooltips in the bottom right give you ideas as to what each item is useful for.

  • Crafting becomes available when you have the resources. E.g. A torch becomes available when you have enough sticks, cloth (webs), vines and oil.

  • There a various different ways to escape the island. Successfully build one and the game is ended. These include a raft, boat, plane and a space ship! :D

Development Tools Used

  • Eclipse IDE

  • Java with the Slick2d library

  • Jarsplice for packaging

  • GitHub for file hosting
