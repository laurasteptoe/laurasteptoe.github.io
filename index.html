Global Game Jam!
23rd Jan 2015

Tonight I’ll be joining 1000s of people from around the world in the Global Game Jam. The aim of the event is to create a game within the 48 hour time period. Before the timer begins I’ll have a chance to meet people to find out who I’d like to work with (or can!). As a team we’ll have the remaining 48 hours to go through every phase of development, from ideas to final game.

The last time I did something similar was last June for Ludum Dare 29 when I created Beneath. Although I thought (personally) it was good, it did feel somewhat lacking in terms of sound. If you’ve played it you’ll know what I mean. Hopefully this time I can work with someone who can make some original music for the team.

Unfortunately I don’t think I’ll be posting live updates so if you’re interested then I think there’ll be live streams of the event here.

Well, hopefully by this time on Sunday I’ll have another complete game to call my work :D
